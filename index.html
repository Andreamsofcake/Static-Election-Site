About


Magistrate Alison Breaux
for Common Pleas Court

People deserve fair and honest justice that enforces our laws and serves our community. My experience as a magistrate and attorney will make me the type of judge Summit County needs.
I’m an Akron resident and I am committed to our community.

As a lawyer, I’ve practiced civil litigation, insurance defense and criminal defense in both large and small firms in the public and private sector. I have represented the big corporation, the accused defendant and everyone in between.
I ruled on thousands of cases as a Magistrate. While on the bench, I have had polished lawyers and everyday people representing themselves in my court – all of whom have been treated the same – with fair and honest justice. No matter what is at stake, everyone deserves equality in the courtroom.

As Common Pleas Judge, I will work tirelessly to find the answers, the right answers, the just answers. I will apply the law to the facts presented and come to a just conclusion.
Since 2012 I have served as a board member for “Life Is Good No Matter What”, an organization dedicated to granting escapes to adults with advanced cancer. This work is not only extremely rewarding, but it has opened my eyes to see how precious life truly is.
Outside of my professional life, I am a wife and dedicated mother of three children, ages 12, 10 and 8.
